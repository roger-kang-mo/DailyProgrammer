This program takes in an poker hand from the user.
Card value first, then suit.
	Ex: AH 9C 5D 8S KC
	    Ace of Hearts, 9 of Clubs, 5 of Diamonds,
	    8 of Spades, King of Clubs

Then, it'll print back out your hand strength such as Royal Flush,
King high, Full House (Jacks over tens), Flush, etc etc.
